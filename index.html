WHY
AURALINE Design ?

At Auraline Design, we don't just craft surfaces—we shape experiences. For over a decade, every texture we've created has told a story, transforming ordinary spaces into extraordinary expressions of design. Whether it's a handcrafted wall panel adding depth to an interior or a designer door skinmaking a bold first impression, our work is about more than aesthetics

Our journey begins with materials—liquid metal, decorative concrete, paintable rust, resin, and more—each carefully chosen for its beauty, durability and versatility. Through a blend of artisanal techniques and modern innovation, we craft surfaces that are as enduring as they are striking, designed for both interiors and exteriors. Whether through bespoke onsite applications or seamless factory finishes, every detail is tailored to perfection.
